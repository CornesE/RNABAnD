We organized the 12th edition of the RNA Club, bringing together researchers to discuss the latest advances in RNA biology.

May 2024 - Open positions available to join the lab.
Funded Ph.D. project and postdoc positions available to join our lab.
- If you are interested in cell differentiation and animal development .
- If you are interested in studying gene regulation from different biological scales .
- If you like to combine molecular approaches with quantitative in vivo cell biology .
- If you want to be part of a new team, contribute to establish new methods and actively participate to lab organization and culture .

What we offer :
1) funded positions.
2) the possibility to develop new research lines, set up collaborations and lead your project.
3) on-site access to state-of-the-art facilities for advanced microscopy, cell and worm sorting, proteomics etc.

-> We always welcome applications for lab internships from students in national and international M1 and M2 master's programs.

Applications should be sent by e-mail to : eric.cornes (at) inserm.fr
and should include:
1) A motivation letter.
2) CV, with the contact information of 2-3 references.
